What is our mission?

Our mission is to fund and foster Translational Research and Development of Biomimetic Concepts

We plan to do this until they are considered commercial candidates with the objective to build a Portfolio of Biomimetic Inspired Platform Technologies. Swedish Biomimetics 3000® is addressing this opportunity by operating under its innovation accelerating model, V²IO®

The model has initially been adopted to support the licensed platform technologies, µMist® and µLot®
