The understanding of how to derive technologies from biological sources has rapidly evolved over the last twenty years

Today biomimetics play a vital part in the future of medicine, materials and manufacturing

Recently, we have seen the optimisation of automatic manufacturing robots (inspired by reptiles), drones (inspired by movement aquatic mammals), robust materials (inspired by spider web fibres) etc

In the consumer sector we have seen Velcro, new adhesives, and frictionless surfaces replicating shark skin make big impacts
