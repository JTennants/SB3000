What is biomimetics?

The term ‘biomimetics’ derives from Ancient Greek as ‘life imitation’: βίος, life and μίμησις, imitation

DESIGN INSPIRED BY NATURE

We look to nature for concepts that may be adopted for technology breakthroughs, social and commercial benefits.

SB3000 AB strive to find, develop and launch biomimetic concepts and technologies inspired by nature and to apply them to today’s environmental and climate change challenges.
